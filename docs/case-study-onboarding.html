Onboarding Insights

An in-depth look at how targeted research and rapid prototyping improved a product’s onboarding experience.

Problem

Users were dropping off during the onboarding flow and reported confusion around key steps. This led to activation rates below expectations and a higher support volume.

Approach

  • Conducted user interviews to identify pain points and mental models.
  • Mapped the current onboarding flow and prioritized improvements.
  • Built mid-fidelity prototypes for key screens and progressive disclosure.
  • Tested prototypes in moderated sessions and iterated based on feedback.

Outcome

  • Completed onboarding completion increased by 18%.
  • Reduced time-to-first-value by simplifying choices and using clearer language.
  • Delivered a set of reusable patterns that aligned with the product’s existing design system.
