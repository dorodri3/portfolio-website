Usability Lab Sprint

A look at how rapid usability testing drove product improvements and reduced support volume.

Problem

The product suffered from high support volume and low task success rates due to usability issues.

Approach

  • Designed a rapid testing plan aligned to the highest-value tasks.
  • Conducted moderated sessions with target users to observe behavior.
  • Created prioritized recommendations and quick-win improvements.

Outcome

  • Task success rate improved by 22% within two weeks.
  • Support volume decreased due to clearer terminology and navigation.
  • Stakeholders aligned on next steps through shared synthesis artifacts.
